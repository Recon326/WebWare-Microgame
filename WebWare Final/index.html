This game only has 2 microgames, it features a lives system, as well as the ability to swap between each game randomly.

The two games are pretty simple, just avoid obstacles, however one is avoiding drivers, while the other uses gravity to avoid the koopas.Mario can jump infinitely.(Trying to work on it)

Controls

W - Jump, Move up

A - Move Left

S - Move down(Driving microgame only)

D - Move right
